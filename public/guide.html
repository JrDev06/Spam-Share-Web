Guide for FB Auto Share

How to Use FB Auto Share

  1. Get Your Facebook Token:

    Navigate to Token Getter to obtain your Facebook Access Token by entering your email and password.

  2. Access Token and Post URL:

    On the main page, enter your Facebook Access Token, the URL of the post you want to share, and the number of times you want to share it.

  3. Submit the Form:

    Click the "Submit" button to start the sharing process. The application will handle the sharing requests as per the specified count.

  4. Monitor and Manage:

    Ensure that your Facebook account remains active and does not get locked. The application will continue to attempt shares until the process is completed or the account becomes inactive.

Troubleshooting

  • Token Issues: Ensure that you are using a valid and active token obtained from the Token Getter page.
  • Share Limits: Verify that the URL you are trying to share is public and accessible.
  • Account Lock: If your Facebook account gets locked, you may need to resolve it manually before resuming the sharing process.
